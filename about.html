About Me

Dianne – occasionally Karla, although I prefer to call as Dianne. I recall the first time I touched and used a DSLR camera. It is bulky. My first impression is that it is difficult to use and, of course, heavy to carry. However, my first attempt at photographing a flower makes me feel as though "I want this!" I recall a time when my partner and I clashed a lot because I did not inform her that I was going to get a DSLR immediately. And I purchased it using our funds. Naturally, I am afraid. However, my heart is content. I am content, and the argument is worthwhile.

I applied as a freelance photographer. I have been so fortunate that I have chosen to become an official photographer for our community's league. It also joined an organisation where I am responsible for photographing, editing, compiling, and submitting images for use as a poster.

But you know, it’s not the end of everything. I want to expand my knowledge in this industry. That’s why I am studying abroad taking up Interactive Media Design. I am not regretting anything right now. I feel like I am in the right part of my life.

About Kliks

Kliks Photography was founded in 2020 while I was considering how I am going to save all my raw and processed images. I am not looking to conceal them all. I want them to share and to know how nice it feels to have this type of passion. Be low are some of my shots. Please feel free to browse them.
